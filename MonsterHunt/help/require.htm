Requirements

MH requires that you have UT patch 436 or higher installed to run.

You can get the latest patches from one of the following sites:

UnrealZA

PlanetUnreal

UnrealTournament.org

Unreal Center

Unreal Files

Unreality

Unreal Engine

Unrealisim

Epic's Unreal Tech site

 

Also, because of the large, richly detailed areas in some maps, some players with low-end systems may notice some slow down. It's therefore recommended that you update your hardware drivers to allow optimum performance.
